Detailed Description

A triangular state constraint preconditioner used in.

Lubkoll2015a.

A preconditioner for optimal control problems of the form

\[ P=\left( \begin{array}{ccc} & & A^{-T} \\ & M^{-1} & -B^T \\ A^{-1} & -B & \end{array} \right)\]

, where \(A\) is the state operator, \(B\) the control operator and \(M\) induces a scalar product on the control space.
