Detailed Description

The affine covariant step method described in Lubkoll2015, Lubkoll2015a for the solution of equality constraint optimization problems.

An affine covariant composite step method for the solution of problems of the form

\[\min f(x)\quad \text{s.t.}\quad c(x)=0\]

, based on the corresponding Lagrange functional

\[L(x,p) = f(x)+pc(x)\]

.
